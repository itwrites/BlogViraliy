Theme Name: NovaPress Magazine
AI-Ready Theme Description (Highly Specific)

NovaPress Magazine is a modern, editorial-style blog theme with a clean, grid-based layout and premium magazine aesthetics. The design uses ample white space, soft shadows, and rounded image cards to create a polished, contemporary look. The overall mood is minimal, high-tech, and professional.

Color Palette

Background: Very light gray (#F5F7FA) or pure white

Accents: Soft blue tones (#3A7BFF – #6EA8FF)

Text: Dark gray (#1A1A1A)

Metadata: Muted gray (#6E6E6E)

Typography

Primary font: Modern sans-serif (e.g., Inter, SF Pro, or similar)

Style: Clean, high-contrast headings with light-weight body text

Article titles: Bold

Metadata: Small, light, spaced out

Header

Full-width top navigation bar with logo centered or left-aligned

Links: Home, Categories, About, Contact

Right side includes search icon, theme switch, and login button

Background: White with subtle shadow

Hero Section

Two-column layout

Left Column:

One large featured article with wide image (16:9)

Title overlay or below image

Short summary in small text

Right Column:

Three vertical stacked article cards

Each card has a square or 4:3 image, small title, and metadata

Category Sections

Each category (e.g., Electronics, Trends) appears as a full-width block with:

Category name on the left

"View all" link on the right

4-card horizontal grid

Cards include:

Rounded image

Title

Small excerpt or metadata

Soft drop shadow

Card Style (Very Specific)

Border radius: 12–16px

Shadow: Subtle, 5–10px soft blur

Hover state: Slight scale-up (1.02) and shadow enhancement

Image crops: Consistent, edge-to-edge

Title: 1–2 lines

Metadata: Date + category in muted gray

Spacing & Layout

Grid gutters: 24px

Section padding: 60–80px top & bottom

Cards use a 3–5 column structure depending on screen width

Very symmetrical and balanced visual rhythm
